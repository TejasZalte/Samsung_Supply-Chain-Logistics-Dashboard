# 📦 Samsung Supply Chain & Logistics Dashboard

## About the Project

This is a Supply Chain & Logistics Dashboard that I built in **Power BI** to understand how different parts of a supply chain work together. The dashboard focuses on important business areas such as revenue, profit, inventory, shipments, suppliers, customers, and product performance.

The main goal of this project was to convert raw data into meaningful insights that can help businesses make better decisions. While building this dashboard, I also improved my understanding of data modeling, DAX, and dashboard design.

## Key Insights

While exploring the data, I found a few interesting insights:

- The business generated **176.95M** in total revenue with a **27% profit margin**.
- **Galaxy S24** turned out to be the best-selling product.
- **Amazon** contributed the highest revenue among all customers.
- The average supplier lead time was around **12 days**, showing fairly consistent supplier performance.
- **Maersk Line** experienced the highest shipment delays, which could be an area to improve logistics operations.
- The inventory contained around **129K units**, helping maintain product availability.
- The business achieved a **75% Perfect Order Rate**, meaning most customer orders were delivered successfully without issues.
